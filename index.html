Our History

Founded in 2023 by Edwin Kumi, EDDTECH Company Limited was established with a mission to provide reliable, accessible, and practical technology solutions across Ghana. What began as a dedicated computer repair shop in Accra has rapidly evolved into a comprehensive IT hub built on professionalism and client satisfaction.


Our Evolution

  • 2023 (The Foundation):Established a reputation for excellence in hardware repairs, software troubleshooting, and network setups, earning trust through honest service and meticulous attention to detail.
  • 2024 (Growth & Expansion):
    • Scaled operations to include network installations, CCTV security, equipment sales, and advanced IT support for businesses and educational institutions.
  • 2025 (Digital Transformation):
    • Launched a digital services division offering website design, SEO, online branding, and mobile app development, positioning the company as a full-scale digital solutions partner.

Our Founder

Edwin Kumi, founder of EDDTECH Company Limited, established the company in 2023 with a vision to deliver reliable and innovative technology solutions. He grew up in Teshie and received his education at Wajir B, Aquinas Senior High School, and Ghana Communication Technology University, where he earned a degree in Information Technology. With strong expertise in IT systems, networking, and software development, Edwin leads EDDTECH with a hands-on approach, ensuring quality service, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
